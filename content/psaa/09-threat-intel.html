IOC Enrichment Workflow

  1. Collect IOC from alert (IP, hash, domain, URL)
  2. Check VirusTotal — community detections + historical context
  3. Check AbuseIPDB for IP reputation and abuse reports
  4. Check URLScan.io for safe site analysis / screenshot
  5. Search OTX AlienVault for related threat campaigns
  6. Map to MITRE ATT&CK technique if known
  7. Document findings in incident ticket
