Cyber Kill Chain — Attacker Progression

Understanding this helps you know WHAT you are looking at in logs and alerts.

PhaseDescription
1. ReconnaissanceAttacker gathers info — OSINT, scanning, enumeration
2. WeaponizationCreates malware or exploit
3. DeliveryPhishing email, USB, web exploit
4. ExploitationExecutes code on victim system
5. InstallationInstalls backdoor for persistent access
6. C2 (Command & Control)Establishes communication channel back to attacker
7. Actions on ObjectivesData theft, ransomware, lateral movement
